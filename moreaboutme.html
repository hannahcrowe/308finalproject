More About Me

In the upcoming spring semester, I'm excited to take on a new role as the Props Chair for my sorority in our school's charity dance competition fundraiser called Greek Sing. This position allows me to merge my passion for design with philanthropy, as I'll be responsible for creating all the stage props and painting backdrops used by the performers. It's a thrilling opportunity to bring together my artistic skills and contribute to a meaningful cause, adding an extra layer of creativity to the intersection of art and service.

After graduating in May 2024, I am eager to embark on my professional journey and secure a full-time position in Richmond, Virginia, focusing on User Experience (UX) design. My goal is to bring my creative flair into the corporate realm, shaping user interactions and crafting meaningful experiences. I am actively applying for positions that align with my passion for design and user-centric principles, anticipating the opportunity to contribute my skills and enthusiasm to a dynamic workplace. Richmond, with its vibrant culture and thriving industry, is where I hope to kickstart my career and make a lasting impact in the field of UX design.
